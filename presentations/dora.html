The Goal: Speed & Stability

Years of research by DORA (DevOps Research and Assessment), published in the book "Accelerate," proved that high-performing organizations don't trade speed for stability—they excel at both. They measure their performance using four key metrics.

The Four Key Metrics

Velocity

How fast we deliver value.

Deployment Frequency: How often do we release to production?

Lead Time for Changes: How long does it take from code commit to production?

Stability

How reliable and resilient our service is.

Change Failure Rate: What percentage of our deployments cause a failure?

Time to Restore Service: When a failure occurs, how fast can we recover?

The Goal is Elite Performance

This isn't just about measurement; it's about creating a culture of continuous improvement driven by objective data.
