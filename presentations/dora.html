The fundamental question for any tech organization is...

How Do We Go Faster...

Finding Answers in Data, Not Feelings

For years, this question was answered with 'gut feel'. Then, a team of researchers did something radical: they applied scientific rigor.

  • DORA (DevOps Research and Assessment) was born.
  • They conducted 6+ years of research across 31,000+ professionals worldwide.
  • Their findings were published in the groundbreaking book, "Accelerate".

They proved that you don't need to trade speed for stability. The best performers excel at both.

So, DORA gives us a map...

"Can you find the bottleneck? Where is the friction point?"

They discovered you only need 4 key metrics.

These metrics create a balanced view, measuring both the **Velocity** of our delivery and the **Stability** of our systems.

Velocity

How fast we deliver value.

+

Stability

How reliable our service is.

VELOCITY METRIC #1

Deployment Frequency

The Core Question: How often do we successfully release to production?

Why it Matters:

This is the pulse of our delivery engine. Higher frequency means smaller, safer changes and faster value to customers.

Elite Performers: On-demand (multiple deploys per day)
VELOCITY METRIC #2

Lead Time for Changes

The Core Question: How long does it take for committed code to get to production?

Why it Matters:

This measures our end-to-end speed. Shorter lead times mean we can iterate faster and respond to customer needs immediately.

Elite Performers: Less than one day
STABILITY METRIC #1

Change Failure Rate

The Core Question: What percentage of our deployments cause a failure in production?

Why it Matters:

This is our primary measure of quality. A low failure rate means our speed is responsible and our processes are effective.

Elite Performers: 0-15%
STABILITY METRIC #2

Time to Restore Service

The Core Question: When a failure occurs, how long does it take us to recover?

Why it Matters:

This measures our resilience. Elite teams can experiment and take risks because they know they can fix any issue rapidly.

Elite Performers: Less than one hour
